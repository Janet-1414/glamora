How It All Started

It began in a small hostel room, with two best friends and a dream.
No equipment, no capital, just big hearts and bold ideas.
From doing makeovers for classmates to running weekend beauty sessions in campus hostels,
Glamora grew from whispered plans into a celebrated beauty brand known for quality, care, and confidence.
